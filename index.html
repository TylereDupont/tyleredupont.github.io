Japan

Japan is one of the greatest countries to make memories and explore culture. Whether you want to explore the night life of Akihabara, or find your spirituality at Fushimi Inari shrine, you're sure to have a great time.

  • 127 million people live in Japan

  • Japan consists of 6,852 islands

  • Japan will host the 2020 Olympics

  • Japan is known as Nihon or Nippon which translates as "Land of the rising sun" It was long ago believed that Japan was the first country to see the sun rise every day.
